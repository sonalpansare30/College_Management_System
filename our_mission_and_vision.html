Our Mission

Vision The vision of the institute is to provide quality higher education to the students of rural and tribal area, aspiring to develop a global perspective in Information Technology and Management Education. Mission

  • To remain outstanding provider of high quality Management and IT education to create proactive and excellent professionals.
  • To impart quality education to students and mould them into responsible persons by raising their level of education and social status.
  • To provide value based education and igniting young minds to bring out the best in them.
  • To make the students competent in global scenario and become self-reliant.
