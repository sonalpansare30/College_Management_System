About R. C. Patel Educational Trust

Since last 15 years R. C. Patel Educational Trust has been performing unique task of imparting education through its branches. R. C. Patel Educational Trust offers excellent facilities for higher education. It runs various renowned Institutes such as.
A Management Institute An Engineering College
A college of Pharmacy A college of Education (B.Ed.)
A college of Diploma in Education (D.Ed.) An Arts, Commerce And Science Senior college

From 1997-98 the trust has started a new institution namely Institute of Management Research & Development for conducting courses leading to the degree and diploma in management discipline and also for advance empirical research under affiliation to North Maharashtra University , Jalgaon.

About Institute

Institute of management Research and Development, Shirpur is one of the premiere institute run by the R.C. Patel Educational Trust, Shirpur. IMRD is situated at the foot of the Satpura ranges and dotted with dotted with lush green trees and shrubs all round it. It has most picturesque and inviting atmosphere.

The R.C. Patel Educational Trust has been existing for 15 years. It has short but hoary past that challenges the pattern of education in present and future. The leadership in the institute has met the challenges with foresight and fortitude and emerged with three key areas being identified as being consistent with their mission academic excellence, spiritual vitality and social concern.
